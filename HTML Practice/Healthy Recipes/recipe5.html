Palak Paneer with Multigrain Roti

Estimated Calories: 450–520 per serving of Palak Paneer (1 cup or around 200 grams) with Multigrain Roti (1 medium roti, ~50 grams)

Health Benefits:

  • Spinach provides iron, calcium, and antioxidants.
  • Multigrain roti is rich in fiber and complex carbs.

Ingredients for Palak Paneer:

  • 2 cups spinach leaves (palak)
  • 100g paneer (cottage cheese), cubed
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, pureed
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp oil
  • Salt to taste

Ingredients for Multigrain Roti:

  • 1/2 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1/4 cup bajra (pearl millet) flour
  • 1/4 cup jowar (sorghum) flour
  • Water for kneading

Instructions for Palak Paneer:

  1. Blanch spinach leaves in boiling water for 2 minutes, then blend into a smooth puree.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, and let them splutter. Add onions and sauté until golden.
  3. Add tomato puree, green chili, turmeric, and garam masala. Cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Add the spinach puree, paneer cubes, and salt. Simmer for 2–3 minutes.

Instructions for Multigrain Roti:

  1. Mix the flours and knead into a soft dough using water.
  2. Roll into thin rotis and cook on a hot tawa until golden spots appear on both sides.
