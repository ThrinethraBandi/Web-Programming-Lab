Quinoa Vegetable Pulao

Estimated Calories: 215–260 per serving (about 1 cup or 200 grams)

Health Benefits:

  • Quinoa is high in protein and contains all nine essential amino acids.
  • Packed with vitamins and minerals from the vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans, etc.)
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 1 green chili, slit
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Rinse quinoa thoroughly and cook it with 2 cups of water until fluffy. Set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, and let them splutter.
  3. Add onions and green chili, sauté until golden. Add vegetables, turmeric, garam masala, and salt. Cook until vegetables are tender.
  4. Add cooked quinoa, mix well, and simmer for 2 minutes.
  5. Garnish with coriander and serve.
