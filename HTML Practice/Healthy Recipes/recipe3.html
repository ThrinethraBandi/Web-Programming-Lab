Vegetable Moong Dal Idli

Estimated Calories: 50–60 per serving of 1 Vegetable Moong Dal Idli (approximately 50–60 grams)

Health Benefits:

  • High in protein and fiber, keeping you full for longer.
  • Low in carbs compared to regular idlis.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup moong dal (split green gram), soaked for 4–6 hours
  • 1/4 cup grated carrots
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped spinach
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp ginger paste
  • 1/2 tsp eno (fruit salt)
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Grind the soaked moong dal into a smooth batter. Add salt, ginger paste, and green chili.
  2. Mix in the grated carrots and chopped spinach. Add water if the batter is too thick.
  3. Just before steaming, add eno to the batter and mix gently.
  4. Grease idli molds and pour the batter into them. Steam for 10–12 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with mint or coconut chutney.
