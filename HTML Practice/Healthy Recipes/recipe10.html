Mixed Vegetable Curry with Foxtail Millet

Estimated Calories: 270–380 per serving of Mixed Vegetable Curry (about 1 cup) with Foxtail Millet (about 1/2 cup or 100g)

Health Benefits:

  • Foxtail millet is gluten-free, high in protein, and aids in weight management.
  • The curry provides a variety of vitamins and minerals from the vegetables.

Ingredients for Mixed Vegetable Curry:

  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (cauliflower, carrots, beans, etc.), chopped
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, pureed
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp oil
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk (optional)
  • Salt to taste

Ingredients for Foxtail Millet:

  • 1 cup foxtail millet
  • 2.5 cups water
  • Salt to taste

Instructions for Vegetable Curry:

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add onions, and sauté until golden.
  2. Add tomato puree, turmeric, cumin, coriander powder, and garam masala. Cook until the oil separates.
  3. Add vegetables, salt, and water. Cover and cook until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Stir in coconut milk (optional) and simmer for 2–3 minutes.

Instructions for Foxtail Millet:

  1. Rinse millet thoroughly and cook with water and salt in a pot or pressure cooker until soft and fluffy.
  2. Serve with the vegetable curry.
