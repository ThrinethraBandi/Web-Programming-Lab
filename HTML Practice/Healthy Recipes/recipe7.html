Stuffed Bajra Paratha with Cucumber Raita

Estimated Calories: 240â€“295 per serving of Stuffed Bajra Paratha (1 medium paratha, around 100 grams) with Cucumber Raita (1/4 cup, around 50 grams)

Health Benefits:

  • Bajra is high in fiber and helps control blood sugar.
  • The yogurt in the raita aids digestion and cools the body.

Ingredients for Paratha:

  • 1 cup bajra (pearl millet) flour
  • 1/4 cup mashed boiled potatoes
  • 1/4 cup grated carrots
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/4 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Water for kneading

Ingredients for Cucumber Raita:

  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 cup grated cucumber
  • 1/4 tsp roasted cumin powder
  • Salt to taste

Instructions for Paratha:

  1. Mix bajra flour with mashed potatoes, grated carrots, cumin powder, chili powder, and salt.
  2. Add water gradually to form a soft dough. Divide into small balls.
  3. Roll each ball into a paratha and cook on a tawa with minimal oil until both sides are golden.

Instructions for Cucumber Raita:

  1. Mix yogurt, grated cucumber, roasted cumin powder, and salt in a bowl. Chill before serving.
