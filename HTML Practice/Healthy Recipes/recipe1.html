Oats Upma

Estimated Calories: 180–200 per serving (1 cup or ~150 grams)

Health Benefits:

  • High in fiber, which aids digestion.
  • Low in calories and supports weight management.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans, etc.)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • Curry leaves
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp oil

Instructions:

  1. Dry roast the oats in a pan for 2–3 minutes and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in the same pan. Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  3. Add green chili, curry leaves, and onions. Sauté until onions turn translucent.
  4. Add the mixed vegetables, turmeric, and salt. Cook until the vegetables soften.
  5. Add the roasted oats and mix well. Add 1/2 cup of water and cook for 2–3 minutes.
  6. Turn off the heat, add lemon juice, and garnish with coriander leaves. Serve warm.
