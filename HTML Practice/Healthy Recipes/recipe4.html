Vegetable Dal Khichdi

Estimated Calories: 200–300 per serving (about 1 cup or 200–250 grams)

Health Benefits:

  • High in protein and fiber.
  • Easy to digest, making it suitable for all ages.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup rice (brown rice for added health benefits)
  • 1/4 cup yellow moong dal (split yellow lentils)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans, etc.)
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp ghee (clarified butter)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the rice and dal thoroughly. Soak for 15–20 minutes.
  2. Heat ghee in a pressure cooker. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add the chopped vegetables and sauté for 2–3 minutes.
  4. Add the soaked rice and dal, turmeric powder, salt, and 2.5 cups of water.
  5. Pressure cook for 3–4 whistles. Let the pressure release naturally.
  6. Garnish with coriander and serve with yogurt or pickle.
