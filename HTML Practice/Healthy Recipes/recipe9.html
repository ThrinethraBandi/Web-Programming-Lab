Vegetable Ragi Upma

Estimated Calories: 215–250 per serving (about 1 cup or 200 grams)

Health Benefits:

  • Ragi is a great source of calcium and iron.
  • The vegetables add essential vitamins and fiber.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup ragi (finger millet) flour
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans, etc.), finely chopped
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 2 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh curry leaves and coriander for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and curry leaves. Let them splutter.
  2. Add onions and green chili; sauté until translucent.
  3. Add the vegetables and cook for 2–3 minutes.
  4. Add water, salt, and bring it to a boil.
  5. Gradually add ragi flour while stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  6. Cover and cook on low heat for 5 minutes.
  7. Garnish with coriander and serve hot.
