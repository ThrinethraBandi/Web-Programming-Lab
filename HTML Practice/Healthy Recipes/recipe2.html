Ragi (Finger Millet) Dosa

Estimated Calories: 85–100 per serving of 1 Ragi Dosa (approximately 60–70 grams)

Health Benefits:

  • High in calcium and iron, beneficial for bone health.
  • A good source of complex carbohydrates and low on the glycemic index.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup ragi flour
  • 1/4 cup rice flour (optional for crispiness)
  • 1/4 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onions and green chilies (optional)
  • Water as needed
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix ragi flour, rice flour, and yogurt. Add water gradually to make a thin, pourable batter. Add salt and let it rest for 15 minutes.
  2. Heat a non-stick pan. Pour a ladle of batter and spread it thinly in a circular motion.
  3. Drizzle a little oil around the edges and cook until crisp. Flip and cook the other side for a few seconds.
  4. Serve hot with mint chutney or sambar.
