Lentil Soup with Whole Wheat Chapati

Estimated Calories: 300–350 per serving (1 cup of soup and 1 chapati)

Health Benefits:

  • Lentils are rich in protein, fiber, and iron.
  • Whole wheat chapati provides sustained energy and is a good source of complex carbohydrates.

Ingredients for Lentil Soup:

  • 1/2 cup lentils (red or yellow), rinsed
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 medium tomato, chopped
  • 1 small carrot, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 4 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Ingredients for Whole Wheat Chapati:

  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • Water, as needed for kneading
  • 1 tsp oil (optional)

Instructions for Lentil Soup:

  1. Heat oil or ghee in a pot. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add onions and garlic, sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add tomatoes, carrots, turmeric, and salt. Cook for 2–3 minutes.
  4. Add rinsed lentils and water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20–25 minutes until lentils are soft.
  5. Blend the soup slightly for a creamy texture or keep it chunky as preferred.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves before serving.

Instructions for Whole Wheat Chapati:

  1. In a bowl, mix whole wheat flour and salt. Gradually add water to form a smooth dough. Let it rest for 15–20 minutes.
  2. Divide the dough into small balls. Roll each ball into a thin circle using a rolling pin.
  3. Cook the chapati on a hot skillet (tawa) until both sides are golden and puffed.
  4. Serve warm with lentil soup.
